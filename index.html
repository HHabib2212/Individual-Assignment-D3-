Interactive Correlation Heatmap

Health Indicators - BRFSS 2024 Dataset

This visualization shows the correlation between various health indicators including BMI, general health, smoking status, exercise, diabetes, and cardiovascular health metrics. Hover over cells to see detailed correlation values.

Variable Descriptions

  • BMI: Body Mass Index
  • General Health: Self-reported general health status
  • Smoking Status: Whether smoked 100+ cigarettes lifetime
  • Smoke Daily: Current smoking frequency
  • Exercise: Any physical activity in past 30 days
  • Diabetes: Diabetes diagnosis status
  • Heart Attack: Ever had heart attack/myocardial infarction
  • Heart Disease: Ever had coronary heart disease
  • Stroke: Ever had a stroke
  • High BP: High blood pressure indicator

Data Source: BRFSS 2024 | Visualization by D3.js
